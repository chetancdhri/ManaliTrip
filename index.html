About The Trip

Discover the magic of Himachal Pradesh with our carefully curated itinerary from Mumbai to Chandigarh.

Why Choose This Trip?

Our Wanderlust Escape tour is the perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and cultural exploration. This 10-day, 9-night trip offers a premium experience in one of India's most beautiful regions.

From the snow-capped mountains of Manali to the spiritual serenity of the Golden Temple, and from thrilling adventure sports to peaceful nature walks - this trip has something for everyone.

Adventure Sports

River rafting, skiing, snow tubing, zipline and ATV rides included

Cultural Sites

Visit historical and spiritual landmarks including Golden Temple and Wagah Border

Trip Itinerary

Detailed day-by-day plan of our amazing 10-day, 9-night Himachal adventure

Day 1: 29th Dec

Departure from Mumbai

Board Garib Rath Express (12909) from Bandra Terminus in the evening for an overnight train journey to Delhi.

Day 2: 30th Dec

Delhi Arrival | Overnight Bus to Amritsar

Arrival at Hazrat Nizamuddin/Sarai Rohilla. Optional Delhi local visit: India Gate, Rajpath, Gurudwara Bangla Sahib. Evening Volvo/Tempo Traveller to Amritsar (8 hrs approx.).

Day 3: 31st Dec

Amritsar: Wagah Border

Hotel check-in and relax. Evening visit to Wagah Border Retreat Ceremony. Special New Years Eve Celebration in Amritsar.

Day 4: 1st Jan

Amritsar Sightseeing | Overnight to Manali

Morning sightseeing: Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh, Partition Museum. Try Amritsari Kuiche for lunch. Free time for shopping. Depart to Manali post-lunch (10-12 hrs drive).

Day 5: 2nd Jan

Manali Arrival & Local Sightseeing

Breakfast at Kullu, Kullu River Rafting (included), Hotel check-in & rest. Explore Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, Manu Temple, Mall Road. Evening Bonfire + DJ Night + Dinner.

Day 6: 3rd Jan

Solang Valley Adventure Day

Full day snow fun at Solang: Skiing, Snow Tubing, Zipline, ATV rides. Optional: Rohtang Pass / Atal Tunnel (weather dependent). Optional Pool Party (if available).

Day 7: 4th Jan

Manali to Kullu Kasol

En route visit: Paragliding Point at own cost, Shawl Factory. Explore Kasol market & cafes. Visit Manikaran Sahib (Hot Springs). Overnight in Kasol (camp/hotel).

Day 8: 5th Jan

Kasol to Shimla

Breakfast & depart early. Scenic 8-9 hour drive to Shimla. Check-in and rest.

Day 9: 6th Jan

Shimla Sightseeing

Full day tour: Kufri Snow Point (Horse Riding, Park), Jakhu Temple, Mall Road, The Ridge, Christ Church. Overnight journey to Chandigarh

Day 10: 7th Jan

Explore Chandigarh

After breakfast. Explore Chandigarh: Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Rose Garden. Drop at Chandigarh station,Board train back to Mumbai from Chandigarh.

Day 11: 8th Jan

Return to Mumbai

End of journey with memories to cherish.
